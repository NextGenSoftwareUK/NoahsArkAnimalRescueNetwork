Spotted stray cats or dogs in your area? Report them here and we'll instantly alert every Noah's Ark member and volunteer in your city so they can help rescue, care for or feed them while space is found.

INSTANT LOCAL ALERT

Your report is broadcast to all volunteers, rescuers and foster carers registered in your city or area — within seconds.

🤝
STREET CARE NETWORK

Volunteers coordinate to feed, shelter and care for strays on the street while a rescue centre place is arranged — no animal left without help.

⚡
EARN KARMA FOR REPORTING

Every verified stray report earns you karma. If the animal is successfully rescued as a result of your report, you earn a rescue bonus too.

🗺️
LIVE STRAY MAP

All active stray reports appear on a live community map so anyone in the area can see where help is needed and coordinate a response.

📸
PHOTO EVIDENCE

Attach a photo to your report so rescuers can identify the animal instantly, assess its condition, and bring the right supplies when they arrive.

🔔
RESCUE UPDATES

Track the outcome of every stray you report. Get notified the moment a volunteer responds, a rescue is confirmed, or the animal finds a home.
